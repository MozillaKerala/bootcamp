Developer Bootcamp

It has been a year since we were formed, after Mozilla Kerala (Meetup 1.0). where 18 passionate Mozillians from across Kerala attended the event. with a common dream - a better Web. We talked, inspired and got inspired. It has been a wonderful time for us - the community - since then. We did a lot of things, organized several events and took part in lots of activities. We taught little kids to code; we taught senior citizens how to use Internet safely; we introduced Firefox OS to developers; and what not!

That was a little brief overview of the community, the idea of bootcamp was born during our last community meetup ( Meetup 2.0), Where we saw there are geeks who can code and willing to contribute to Open projects, but how to get started was an obstacle with them, so we planned to have a even that helps them boot up their dream. Then Initial plan is to take 20 geeks who code and introduce them to tools and other materials that is involved in community build projects like Mozilla, Linux ....

To make it productive we have restrited the attendees to 20. The event includes a night full of hacking too.

Sounds interesting?
